# Marketing Domain Skills

This container houses the marketing and sales team agents.

## Agents in This Domain

| Agent | Role | Primary Skills |
|-------|------|----------------|
| Marketing | Content creation, campaigns, branding | Copywriting, campaign design |
| Sales | Customer outreach, deal tracking | Sales copy, proposals |
| Liaison | Inter-container communication | A2A coordination |

## Common Workflows

1. **Feature Launch**: Receive from dev → Marketing creates copy → Sales prepares outreach
2. **Campaign**: Marketing designs → Sales executes → Report to CxO
3. **Customer Feedback**: Sales collects → Routes to dev or CxO
